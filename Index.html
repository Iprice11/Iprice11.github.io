The Glorification Of the Trap, 2018

The Glorification Of the Trap is projected on the floor to expand on the concept that people outside of hip-hop culture look down on the image of a thug/drug dealer. The video itself is a projection of a few glorified actions that many rappers and other members of hip-hop culture glorify, The Hustle.

LITD Challenege, 2018

The LITD Challenege was a concept inspired by the current living conditions in San Jose the LITD challenge was a experimental documentary on post good deeds on social media.
